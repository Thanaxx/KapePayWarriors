    • WHAT'S WITH US?

    Welcome to MedCare! MedCare provides you an online checkup/medical examination with the guidance of a doctor which is available on that time or day. MedCare Allows you to talk to the doctors as if it's like a face to face checkup wherein there's a live talking with the doctor. This MedCare is hasslefree on going to the hospital in this time of pandemic, also this MedCare is a timely relevant website that can be used for your future needs. The idea of making this MedCare is to bring you a doctor to guide and examine you even though you're at home you just need only to sign up and provide your details for the records, then call the doctor. After this pandemic MedCare will still bring you the services especially for those people that are really busy, persons with disabilities that's hard to travel to go to the hospitals and even to some grandparents that can't walk anymore. This MedCare is free to access all you need is to have a stable internet connection when doing an online checkup to get better advice and right prescription.The advantage of MedCare is that it’s accessible wherever or whenever you are.
